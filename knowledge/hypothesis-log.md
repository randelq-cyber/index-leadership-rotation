# Hypothesis Log

## H1 — Leadership divergence with delayed laggard catch-up
When SPY provides broad-market confirmation and one of QQQ or DIA shows leadership while the other lags, the laggard may outperform the other non-SPY ETF over the next 2 to 5 trading days once a catch-up trigger appears.

### Current assumptions
- Important highs: 60, 128, 252 trading days
- SPY is a context / confirmation filter, not the outcome benchmark
- Outcome comparison is QQQ vs DIA
- Trigger timing should not be defined purely by elapsed days
- First pass should test a small curated trigger set

### Open questions
- Best definition of "leaders are strong right now"
- Best definition of laggard non-confirmation
- Best trigger family for the phase change
- Whether the edge is concentrated in specific market regimes
